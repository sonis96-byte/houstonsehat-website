Pakistan-U.S. Connect

A Shared Global Playbook

Starting with a data linkage between Houston and Pakistan, the team will create an exportable global playbook for future partnerships across South Asian regions.

Program Overview

This initiative examines how migration, environment, culture, and health-system context shape cardiovascular risk across South Asian populations.

The goal is to build a model that starts locally, learns globally, and returns practical prevention strategies to communities.
