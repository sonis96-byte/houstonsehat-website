SAHARA

South Asian Heart-Health Analysis and Risk Assessment

Hearing the Population's Signal

This project analyzes cardiovascular data from more than 30,000 South Asian patients in the Houston Methodist system to identify early, often silent disease markers.

Program Overview

SAHARA forms the scientific backbone of Houston-SEHAT by defining local disease patterns, identifying gaps in prevention, and informing clinical decision-making.

The work supports tailored prevention strategies for South Asian patients across Greater Houston and beyond.

Key Focus Areas

Data PlatformLarge-scale EHR and cardiovascular data
Risk SignalsEarly and silent markers of disease
Clinical TranslationActionable prevention strategies
